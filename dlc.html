About DLC

What is DLC?

DLC stands for District Leadership Conference. At DLC, Mo-Ark Key Club members can get together, attend various forums, participate in contests, and celebrate a year of service — as well as elect new members to the board. It's the highlight of the Key Club year in the Missouri-Arkansas District.
