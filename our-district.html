Our District Board

The Missouri-Arkansas District Board oversees all 10 divisions. Board members are elected annually at DLC and serve throughout the year.

The district board is responsible for overseeing all 10 divisions in the district. Board meetings are held throughout the service year where members discuss the district bylaw, work in committees, and plan the District Leadership Conference. For more information about DLC, visit the DLC page.
